ABOUT US

Flowers4u is the Number one urban landscaping and gardening social enterprise that transforms spaces and communities using plants and flowers in Nigeria. Flowers4u uses various plants and flowers to create beauty within a landscape, fostering appreciation and inspiring stewardship of the environment. We have an incredible selection of vibrant and elegant hand tied bouquets. Our flower delivery service runs 7 days a week and next day delivery option is possible when you order flowers before 10 pm with our flowers by post. Our wide range of fresh flowers and flower gifts ensure that every occasion and personal floral preference is covered.

Vision Statement:

To pioneer and lead the active transformation of urban spaces and connect people to the world of plants.

Mission Statement:

Flowers4u is committed to working with governments, communities and individuals to promote the conservation, display, and enjoyment of plants through landscaping and building of green spaces, community parks, green walls and more, to foster beautiful and eco-friendly communities across the country.
